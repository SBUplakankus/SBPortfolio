About The Game

- NCAA Basketball 18 was my first ever project for NBA 2K
- It took me nearly a year to create the first version from 2017 to 2018
- I had no idea what I was doing so was learning constantly as I progressed
- I had to research over 350 individual players to recreate them in game
- The game features 'mid-major' colleges as they had not been created before
- I recreated most of the art at a higher quality in 2020

Features

- A new take on MyCareer known as ROAD TO GLORY
- All-Patriot League Teams
- All-Ivy League Teams
- Accurate statistics and details for every single player
- CBS Presentation and Scoreboard
- Custom Game Mode names such as March Madness and Legacy Mode
