About The Game

- The G-League Update conversion mod for NBA 2K11 on PC
- It replaces the in-game D-League from 2010
- The D-League feature in 2K11 is amazing and I wanted to improve on it
- I spent a couple months making this in 2018 and released in 2019
- I released one major update that improved all of the courts
- It is still being used today as part of the yearly update mods

Features

- Overhauls the old D-League teams to current G-League teams
- Unlocks the ability to play as G-League Teams in quick-play
- Generic D-League players replaced with real in-game Free Agents
- 4KHD Courts for every team
- Logos and Jerseys for each team as of 2019
- Compatible with every game mode
