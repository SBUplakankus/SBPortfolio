Course Work

- Computer Illustrated Graphics
- Games Design and Analysis
- Games Design and Art
- 3D Modelling and Animation
- Digital Editing
- Web Authoring

I worked in the Adobe suite using applications such as Premiere Pro, Illustrator and Photoshop, while also creating games in software such as Scratch and the Godot Engine. Koala is one of these games which you can play below.
