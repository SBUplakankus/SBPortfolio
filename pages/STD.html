Spud Tower Defence

Unreleased

About The Game

- Spud Tower Defence is my first attempt at making a game in Unity
- I created all the assests in the game myself in Blender
- The game is coded in C# which I have been learning while making this
- I began making the game in January 2023 and it is currently on hold
- I also created alot of 2D art for the game from menus to the HUD to icons

Features

- It's Famine 2.0 as potatoes have sought to bring death to Ireland once more
- Get in your tractors and defend your farm from the spud hoard
- Buy and upgrade your turrets as the waves get tougher and tougher
- Set your tactics from the top down view then enter the battle cam to get up close
- Bring peace back to our beautiful lands
