About The Game

- College Hoops 2K is a total conversion mod for NBA 2K11 on PC
- It features 30 of the top division one schools in place of NBA teams
- I started work on this game in 2018 and released it in early 2019
- I had two major updates for the game after release
- One updated the quality of all courts and jerseys, the other for the players
- This was my first major project done on NBA 2K11

Features

- A fully functioning NCAA MyCareer
- NCAA themed Menus and Presentation
- Working NCAA Association Mode
- 30 HD Courts and Arenas
- Faces and Portraits for every player in the game
- A College Hoops 2K8 feel brought to PC
